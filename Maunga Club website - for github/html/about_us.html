Welcome to the Maunga Club!

 

Maunga Club is a club that offers exclusive access to Snow Mountain and cozy ski chalets!

The mountain provides 550 hectares of skiing and snowboarding area, with the longest ski and snowboard season in New Zealand!

As a member of the Maunga Club, you will have access to our chalets, which offer shared accommodation for between 6 and 40 people per chalet. You can ski to and from the chalets, making it easy to hit the slopes whenever you want!

Only Club members can access the chalets! If you also want to enjoy a comfortable and exciting skiing trip, click "Join us" or click the email below! Just a few simple questions, we will never leak your privacy!

All chalet bookings need to be from Friday to Sunday.

Join us at the Maunga Club for an unforgettable skiing experience!
